Why ElevenLabs
I already use your stuff in production. My portfolio has an ElevenLabs voice agent answering questions about my work and ElevenLabs TTS narrating my GitHub stats - both automated, both live. The Automations Engineer role is literally what I do every day, except I've been doing it for myself.

I miss the energy of a sharp team shipping fast and riffing off each other. ElevenLabs has that energy. I want to bring what I've built solo into a team that matches the pace.
